my work: books & company

Books & Company

Click here to check out the site

The goal of this project was to create a typography based website for a local Hamden bookstore, Books & Company.

The challenge: Lack of information and images available online because the bookstore's only Internet exposure was a Facebook page with limited images.

To gain the necessary information I visited the bookstore multiple times for observations, interviews, inspiration, and to take pictures.

Programs used: Illustrator, Dreamweaver
