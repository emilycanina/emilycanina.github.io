Books & Company

Click here to check out the site.

The goal of this project was to create a typography based website for a local Hamden bookstore, Books & Company. The challenge was the lack of information and images available online because the bookstore's only Internet exposure was a Facebook page with limited images. To gain the necessary information I visited the bookstore multiple times for observations, interviews, inspiration, and to take pictures. It was a great experience, especially to be reliant on mostly primary research.
