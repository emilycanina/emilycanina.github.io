Clean Water for Haiti Website Redesign

Click here to check out the site

The intent of this project was to create a website for the non-profit organization, Clean Water for Haiti.

The challenge was to create a both visually appealing and influential site, while condensing the over welcoming amount of information.

An additional optional task was to redesign the logo.

Programs used: Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, Dreamweaver
