About Us

Who We Are

Clean Water for Haiti opened itʼs doors in Haiti in 2001 and aims to provide the people of Haiti with a way to filter water in their own homes

Who We Work With

Some Of the Organizations We Have Trained: AMURT (Ananda Margda Universal Relief Team) • ACTS • World Vision • Haiti Foundation of Hope • Mission of Hope/Convoy of Hope • All Hands Volunteers • World Neighbors/Vwazsen Mondyal

Our Vision

To demonstrate our love for the Lord and our neighbors by providing support and expertise for sustainable water projects in Haiti.

Our Mission

This mission is dedicated to responding to the needs of the Haitian people in the area of availability to clean water, using simple technology (Biosand water filters), and proven effective methods for development of

Our Goals & Objectives

Provide and install Biosand water filters in communities throughout Haiti.
Establish new production sites to reach new regions of Haiti.
Teach necessary sanitation and hygiene education to filter recipients and their communities.
Create employment opportunities for Haitian men and women.
Support the Haitian economy by purchasing materials locally for filter production and installation.
Train, support and resource other organizations interested in starting a Biosand filter project.

Accountability

When you choose to give to Clean Water for Haiti we know it's because you see the value of the work we do. We believe in being financially accountable to those who make it possible for us to do the work we do. We want to be good stewards of the funds you entrust to us, to be as effective as possible, to make sure our employees are getting paid a fair wage and to making the greatest impact we can with what we have.
