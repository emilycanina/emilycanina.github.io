Donate

None of what we do in Haiti would be possible without the generous gifts of our donors. We aim to be good stewards of the funds we receive and to be accountable to those that give. If you would like to support Clean Water for Haiti financially you can do so through one of the following methods. No donation is too small.

Donate Online

Clicking on one of the following links will take you to a secure donation page.

Give Directly

Clean Water for Haiti is now a registered foundation in Haiti. Registered charities, non-profits and foundations can now send donations directly to the foundation in Haiti. For more information please contact Chris Rolling at info@cleanwaterforhaiti.org.
