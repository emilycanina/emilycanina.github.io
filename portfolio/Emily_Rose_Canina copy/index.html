My Resume

Hello!

My name is Emily Rose Canina. A little about myself, I am a Boston local, I love to be active, to be part of a team, and to problem solve. As a part of a large extended Italian family, I always take with me the core values of family, loyalty and dedication. I enjoy traveling and exploring new places. The spring semester of my junior year I studied abroad in Florence and travelled around the beautiful country. As a graphic designer I'm eager to learn everything and anything about the field. UX design and mobile apps have recently sparked my interest. This past May 2013 I graduated from Quinnipiac University with a BA in Interactive Digital Design and a Minor in Marketing. During my four years in Bobcat Nation, I was an active member of the QU community, participating in a variety of intramurals, organizations, and events on campus. I've always loved being part of a team; even from a young age I partook in team sports and activities and will always be a part of my life.

I hope you enjoy my online portfolio!
