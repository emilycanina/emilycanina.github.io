Clean Water for Haiti Website Redesign

Click here to check out the site

The intent of this project was to create a website for the non-profit organization, Clean Water for Haiti. The challenge was to create a both visually appealing and influential site, while condensing the over welcoming amount of information. An additional optional task was to redesign the logo. For me, it was difficult to implement my designs and put my ideas on paper or even on the computer. Once I fully developed my drafts as if final, I was able to create a more successful and stronger design.
