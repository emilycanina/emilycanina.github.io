my work: grocer app

Grocer App

The goal of this assignment was to design a mobile app first in groups then indivdually.

The project entailed: background research, brainstorming, user testing, focus groups, surveys, observation, storyboarding, information architecture, personas, presenting weekly, and designing the app in fireworks.

Programs used: Survey Monkey, InDesign, Illustrator, POP, & Fireworks.
