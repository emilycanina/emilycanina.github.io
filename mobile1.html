Grocer App

The goal of this assignment was to design a mobile app. The project entailed, background research, brainstorming, user testing, focus groups, surveys, observation, storyboarding, information architecture, personas, presenting weekly, and designing the app in fireworks. The research portion was done in small groups. The app design and final was done individually. This project also required us to use a variety of software, such as Survey Monkey, InDesign, Illustrator, POP, and Fireworks. I thoroughly enjoyed doing this project, and discovered a newfound passion for UX and mobile apps. Enjoy!
